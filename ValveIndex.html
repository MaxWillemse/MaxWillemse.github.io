Valve index

The Valve Index was first released in 2019 and sold an estimated 149,000 sets the same year.
HTC and Valve worked together on making the Valve Index, and made it backwards compatible with any other HTC related VR equipment.
The Valve index originally released along side the long anticipated Half:Life Alyx, wich Valve had been working on for over 7 years
